About Me

Through 9 years of building software to solve a variety of problems, I have evolved into a full-stack engineer with a focus on backend services who also enjoys working in DevOps.

As time has passed I have developed a healthy addiction to learning and frequently seek out new challenges in an attempt to add to my toolbox.

I love helping others learn and I have over 10 years of tutoring experience doing so.

Team process is something I have found to be crucial to the success of a team and I frequently find myself reflecting on how my team is currently operating and suggesting improvements when necessary.

Experiences
  • Using Kubernetes, Docker, and GCP, helped with operations and support of engineering team.
  • Planned team's sprints, including prioritizing weekly work, handling with blockers, and running SCRUM meetings.
  • Migrated our tutor jobs board from .NET MVC to a Ruby on Rails client that leveraged an existing API.
  • Translated and improved an existing .NET payment processing service into Golang.
  • Designed and implemented a Golang Elasticsearch service enabling users to search for questions.
  • Provide code reviews and share my knowledge with our teammates.
  • Create SumoLogic alerts and dashboards to be used for monitoring.
  • Organize Engineering department Hack Day and Happy Hour events.
